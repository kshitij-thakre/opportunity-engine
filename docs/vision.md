# Opportunity Engine - Product Vision

## Executive Summary
Opportunity Engine is an automated discovery and conversion platform built specifically for freelancers, boutique agencies, and MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises). By monitoring digital spaces, public bidding boards, social networks, and enterprise procurement portals, the platform discovers, qualifies, tracks, and helps convert high-value client opportunities into active contracts.

## The Problem
For small services businesses, client acquisition is highly fragmented, time-consuming, and inconsistent:
1. **Scattered Opportunities**: Jobs, RFPs, and consulting requests are spread across dozens of freelance platforms, public procurement boards, and social media sites.
2. **Low Signal-to-Noise Ratio**: Sifting through unqualified leads and low-budget requests consumes valuable billable hours.
3. **Manual Overhead**: Crafting personalized proposals and managing client pipelines manually limits the throughput of small firms.

## The Solution
Opportunity Engine provides an automated workflow to streamline client acquisition:
- **Aggregated Discovery**: Unified intelligence gathering across diverse channels (job sites, RFPs, public bids).
- **Intelligent Qualification**: Semi-automated scoring based on budget, technology alignment, and project complexity.
- **Convert & Close**: Streamlined workflows to generate initial proposals and manage client communications from qualification to contract.

## Target Audience
- **Freelancers & Consultants**: Individuals looking for high-value contracts.
- **Boutique Agencies**: Dev shops, design agencies, and marketing agencies searching for mid-market clients.
- **MSMEs**: Small enterprises bidding on public sector or corporate contracts.
